Chamaedorea

Also known as Parlor Palm, Chamedorea Elegans need bright but indirect light. Parlor palms are sensitive to overwatering and cannot tolerate being waterlogged but need their soil to be kept moist. Parlor Palm in natural habitat, 6 to 16 feet tall; as a houseplant, 2 to 6 feet tall, 2- to 3-foot spread. This plant is non-toxic for animals and humans.
