JADE GARDEN NURSERY

About Us

Jade garden nursery is a commercial nursery engaged in production and selling of ornamental plants and other nursery products. This nursery was established in January 14, 2013.

The CEO of this nursery is Bijaya lama. Jade garden nursery is located in Kathmandu, Nepal. Jade garden nursery supplies all kinds of plants like indoor, outdoor, flowering plants, succulents, seasonal, herbs, shrubs, orchids, trees and plant accessories lie pots, ceramic vase, hanging buckets, clay pots, propagating vessels.

“Gardening adds years to your life, and life to your years” is what this nursery believes. Empowering all people to be plant people and add a plant to their space.

Why Choose Us?

Jade garden Nursery is dedicated to your success. We help your landscape design plans come to life with the finest plants available.

Let us help you meet your client’s requests by procuring everything from common plants to hard-to-find specimens from our growing grounds or extensive network of vendors.

We aim to serve you with respect and honesty, offering the best and most efficient service so you can get your job done on time.
